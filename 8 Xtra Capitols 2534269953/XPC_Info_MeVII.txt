- Summary
-------------------------------
This mod makes capitols a significant place of great power in an empire.
Conquering capitols, along with defending your own,
now have a much greater strategic importance.
_______________________________
- Compatibility Information
-------------------------------

This Mod requires UCP, 5X, and UCP5X Compatibility mods.
(It will function without Custom 5X and DSMP Assets,
however, some game images will be blank)
It should always be loaded after (below) UCP, 5X, and UCP5X Compatibility mods.

--- Files Modified
The following yaml files are used by this mod.
ColonyStructureTypes.yaml

foodTileBonus, foodCarryoverRatio, totalFoodMultiplierBonus, maxPopulationBonus,
populationGrowthMultiplierBonus, moraleMultiplierBonus, moraleMultiplierBonusSystem,
baseProductionBonus, productionTileBonus, workerYieldMultiplierBonus, pollutionCleaningRate,
pollutionMultiplierReduction, baseResearchBonus, researchTileBonus, workerResearchBonus,
securityBonus, systemSecurityBonus, empireSecurityBonus, spyTrainingTimeModifier, startingSpyLvl,
assimilationRateModifier, hasScanner, scannerRangeBonus, fleetSupportBonus, shipCostModifier,
fleetSupportCostReduction, planetDefense, empireUnique, systemUnique

-> It will conflict with / overwrite this information
